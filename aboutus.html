Building Trust in Academic Credentials

EduChain harnesses blockchain and decentralized identity to make certificate verification instantaneous, secure, and fraud-proof.

Our Mission

We believe every learner deserves credentials that are universally trusted. EduChain empowers academic institutions to issue tamper-proof certificates, and enables employers or other verifiers to confirm authenticity with a single click. By eliminating manual checks and paperwork, we cut verification costs and fight academic fraud at its root.

Core Technologies

Blockchain

Immutable ledger on Ethereum securely stores certificate hashes.

Smart Contracts

Solidity contracts automate certificate issuance and verification logic.

Decentralized Identity

DIDs give institutions and students self-owned, verifiable identifiers.

Verifiable Credentials

W3C-standard VCs let any third party cryptographically verify certificates.

IPFS Storage

Decentralized file storage keeps original certificate PDFs tamper-resistant.

How It Works

1️⃣ An institution hashes a PDF certificate and stores the hash on-chain.
2️⃣ The student receives a verifiable credential linked to their decentralized identity.
3️⃣ A verifier uploads the certificate; EduChain recomputes the hash and checks it against the blockchain in seconds. If it matches, authenticity is proven.
